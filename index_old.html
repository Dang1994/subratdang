About

Subrat Kumar is a researcher at the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering Department (MIED), IIT Roorkee, working under Dr. Vidit Gaur. His research focuses on developing next-generation AI-driven systems for advanced materials discovery and design, with emphasis on multimodal learning, large language models (LLMs), and agentic AI frameworks for superalloy design. Before joining IIT Roorkee, he worked for 2.5 years at the Bremen Institute for Production and Logistics (BIBA), University of Bremen, Germany, in the IKAP department under Prof. Klaus-Dieter Thoben and Dipl.-Inform. Karl Hribernik, where he contributed to three major Industry 4.0 (UPMASS, WASABI, TOKIOS) projects funded by Horizon Europe, EIT Manufacturing, and BMBF. He holds an M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from SLIET, India, where he completed his master’s thesis on data-driven manufacturing.
