## About


Subrat Kumar is a researcher at the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering Department (MIED), IIT Roorkee, working under Dr. Vidit Gaur. His research focuses on developing next-generation AI-driven systems for advanced materials discovery and design, with emphasis on multimodal learning, LLMs, and agentic AI frameworks for superalloy design.


Before joining IIT Roorkee, he worked at BIBA, University of Bremen (Germany) contributing to Horizon Europe and BMBF projects. He holds an M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from SLIET, India.
