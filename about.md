<section>
  <h2>Overview</h2>

  <p class="lead">
    Subrat Kumar Dang is a doctoral researcher in the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering Department (MIED) at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, supervised by Dr. Vidit Gaur. His research develops advanced artificial intelligence methodologies to accelerate materials discovery and design.
  </p>

  <p>
    His work emphasizes the integration of physics-informed learning, multimodal representation learning, and agentic AI frameworks to support the systematic exploration of complex materials design spaces while maintaining scientific interpretability and reliability.
  </p>

  <p>
    Prior to joining IIT Roorkee, he contributed to research and development at the Bremen Institute for Production and Logistics (BIBA), University of Bremen, participating in collaborative Industry 4.0 and digital-manufacturing projects. He holds an M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from SLIET, India.
  </p>
