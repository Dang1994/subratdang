About

I am a researcher at the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering Department (MIED), IIT Roorkee, working under Dr. Vidit Gaur. My research focuses on multimodal learning, large language models (LLMs), and agentic AI frameworks tailored to superalloy design and microstructure–property predictions.
