  <h2>Research Summary</h2>

  <p class="lead">
    My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and materials science. I develop computational methods that combine data-driven models with domain knowledge to accelerate the discovery, characterization, and design of high-performance materials.
  </p>

  <div class="grid-3" style="margin-top:16px;">
    <div class="project-card">
      <strong>Agentic AI for Materials Discovery</strong>
      <p class="lead" style="margin-top:8px;">Autonomous agent pipelines that generate hypotheses, schedule simulations, and prioritize candidates using learned utility functions.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="project-card">
      <strong>Physics-Informed Machine Learning</strong>
      <p class="lead" style="margin-top:8px;">Surrogate models and PINNs that embed physical constraints to improve extrapolation and enforce thermodynamic consistency.</p>
    </div>

  <h3 style="margin-top:22px;">Methods & Tools</h3>
  <p>
